OUR HISTORY

The DRX Valorant team, established in 2020, is the first Valorant esports team in Korea and a historic team representing both Korea and Asia. With the incredible achievement of securing 102 consecutive wins, they are recognized as the most creative team in the world, showcasing that a Korean FPS team can also gain global recognition. We always aim for victory and continually strive and challenge ourselves to become stronger than any other team at the top of the world.
